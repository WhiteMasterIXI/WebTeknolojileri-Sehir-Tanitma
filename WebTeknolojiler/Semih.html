Semih Saygıner

Saygıner, who won the first World Billiards Championship in 1994, is known worldwide as "Mr. Magic" or "The Turkish Prince". He is the person who ensured that billiards became a federation in Türkiye. Saygıner, who played professionally in the Dutch league for 9 years and in the Portuguese league for 3 years, has 42 special shot techniques that have been recorded in billiards literature as "Semih Saygıner Magic Shots" and is one of the most important names in the world in the sport of billiards.
