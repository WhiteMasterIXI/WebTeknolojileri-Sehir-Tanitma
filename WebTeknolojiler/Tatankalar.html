Tatankalar

Sakarya Tatankas is the American Football team of Sakarya University. The team entered the establishment process in the fall of 2002 as a result of the optimistic work of Sakarya University students, and carried out basic establishment activities such as forming the team, exchanging ideas and obtaining information in 2002-2003.
