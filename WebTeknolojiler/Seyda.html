Şeyda Burucu

Hafsa Şeyda Burucu is a European champion Turkish karateka competing currently in the kumite -68 kg division. She is a member of Sakarya Büyükşehir Belediyespor Club. Şeyda Burucu became European champion at the 2012 European Karate Championships held in Adeje, Tenerife, Spain.
